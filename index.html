Mischievous Brews For Adventurous You

At Rapscallion Brewing, we take personality seriously. That's why our mission is to bring you unique that rocks your world, one tastebud at a time. We know that when you work hard you deserve to play hard.

Established in Grand Rapids, MI in 2019, it's been a rough road for our little brewpub. If we've learned anything, it's that the people in our community are family, and that we won't quit no matter the odds. You don't quit on family. You make them amazing beer.
